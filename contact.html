Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay securely using credit card, PayPal, or other payment methods.
What are your delivery hours?
We deliver 7 days a week from 8AM to 8PM. You can select your preferred delivery slot during checkout.
Do you offer organic products only?
Yes! All our products are 100% certified organic. We work directly with farmers to ensure the highest quality standards.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with any product, contact us for a full refund or replacement.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking link. You can also track orders in your account dashboard.
Do you have a loyalty program?
Yes! Join our Stackly Rewards program to earn points on every purchase, get exclusive discounts, and enjoy member-only perks.
